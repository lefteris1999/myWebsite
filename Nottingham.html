Key Projects

Augmented Reality Business Card

As part of a collaborative project with IBM, our team developed an Augmented Reality Business Card aimed at redefining traditional networking. Designed to replace the old-fashioned paper business card, this digital alternative allows users to access and interact with dynamic information in an engaging way.

Leveraging Unity for development and Vuforia for AR integration, we crafted an immersive experience where users could view and interact with personalized content via their mobile devices. 3D avatars were designed in Blender, allowing us to incorporate custom animations and effects that enhanced the user experience. The final product enabled users to seamlessly scan the card and access interactive information, showcasing professional details in a way that is both professional and fun, while also minimising environmental impact.

This project gave us practical experience in cross-functional collaboration and equipped us with valuable skills in AR application development.
