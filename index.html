About Me

Hello I’m an aspiring Data Scientist with a passion for transforming data into actionable insights and building innovative, data-driven solutions. I hold a BSc in Computer Science and an MSc in Data Science, where I honed my skills in machine learning, data analytics, and problem-solving.

My key area of interest lies in Natural Language Processing (NLP)—exploring the power of language to unlock valuable patterns and insights. I’m particularly excited about leveraging NLP to tackle real-world challenges, whether it’s sentiment analysis, text classification, or conversational AI.

Always eager to learn and grow, I’m driven to stay at the forefront of data science trends and create impactful solutions that make a difference. Let’s connect and explore the world of data together!

Feel free to explore my portfolio below to see some of my recent projects, and learn about my education and career.
